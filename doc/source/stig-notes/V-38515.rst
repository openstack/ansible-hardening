V-38515: The Stream Control Transmission Protocol (SCTP) must be disabled unless required.
------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Disabling SCTP protects the system against exploitation of any flaws in its
implementation.

Details: `V-38515 in STIG Viewer`_.
